About

A short version, because nobody reads the long one.

I grew up in Harrisburg, South Dakota and spent most of high school chasing college basketball. After my senior state tournament I changed course, applied to IU, and chose Bloomington partly because I didn’t know anyone there — growth happens faster when you’re a little uncomfortable.

I’m an AI Training Intern at Handshake, where I evaluate model behavior and write tests for AI search tools. On campus I’m in Phi Gamma Nu, Black Men Evolve, the Asset Management Club, and SEO Markets Edge.

Long term, I want to work in tech and build a real understanding of how products and markets actually move. I think a lot about what it means to be different on purpose — people like Pharrell are a north star for that.

Honest weakness: I’m bad at staying organized. I like thinking about a lot of things at once, and sometimes the wires cross.
